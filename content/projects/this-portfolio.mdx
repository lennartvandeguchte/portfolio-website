## The Problem

Most developer portfolios look the same — grid of cards, particle hero, Three.js sphere. The challenge was building something that felt genuinely crafted, not assembled from a template.

## The Approach

Typography as the interactive medium. Instead of reaching for WebGL, the site makes words move. GSAP + SplitType split every title into individual characters. Each letter scatters, assembles, and responds to the user.

## The Result

A portfolio that earns a second look. The kinetic type rewards exploration — the longer you interact, the more alive it feels.
